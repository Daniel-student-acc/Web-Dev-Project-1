Why Visit Kirkcaldy

Kirkcaldy is small but beautiful town found on the East Caost of Scotland. It finds itself looking across the Firth o' Fourth at Ediburgh. The town features many interesting places in an around it. Being located, somewhat, centrally between large cities Dundee, Edinburgh, Dunfermline, and Perth. Glasgow is alwo within reasonable distance.
